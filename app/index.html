Scientific Journal

Journal is an international peer-reviewed, partly open-access journal that appears in both digital and print format. It aims to take scientific, and return it to the forefront of conceptual developments in the discipline.

Free access journal

The University publishes one free-access journal: HAU: Journal. This model provides one month of free access after the release of each new issue, and then requires a subscription for continuous access to content. content published from 2011-2017 is open access.
